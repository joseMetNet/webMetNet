# Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how METNET S.A.S. (“METNET,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies on the METNET website.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your browser or device when you visit a website. Similar technologies may include local storage, tags, pixels, scripts, or similar tools used to remember preferences, enable functions, analyze traffic, or improve performance.

2. Types of Cookies We May Use

a. Strictly Necessary Cookies
Required for core website functionality, security, navigation, form submission, session management, and fraud prevention.

b. Functional Cookies
Used to remember preferences such as language or display settings.

c. Performance or Analytics Cookies
Used to understand how visitors use the website and to improve performance and usability.

d. Marketing Cookies
If enabled, these may be used to support campaign measurement, audience segmentation, or relevant communications.

3. How We Use Cookies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to:

keep the website secure and operational;

remember user preferences;

understand usage patterns and improve the site;

measure the effectiveness of communications or campaigns;

support troubleshooting and performance optimization.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be placed by third-party providers we use for hosting, analytics, embedded content, CRM, or performance monitoring.

5. Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through:

the cookie banner or consent tool on the website, where implemented;

your browser settings to block or delete cookies;

non-essential cookie opt-out options where available.

Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality or user experience of the website.

6. Consent

Where required, we will request your consent before placing non-essential cookies.

7. Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect legal, technical, or operational changes.
